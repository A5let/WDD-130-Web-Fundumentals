Overview

Purpose

The DEFCON 2025 Information Site will serve as a centralized hub for attendees, enthusiasts, and media professionals seeking details about the upcoming DEFCON convention scheduled for August 7 - 10, 2025. The site will provide key event information, schedules, FAQs, and guidance to ensure a smooth experience for all visitors.

Audience

All those that are intrested in the information of the upcoming Defcon happening in 2025
