DEFCON 2025 Challenges

Test Your Skills

Participate in Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, lockpicking workshops, and social engineering exercises. Compete against the best in the field and push your limits in these real-world hacking scenarios.

Capture The Flag (CTF)

Test your hacking skills in our premier CTF competition. Teams compete to find vulnerabilities, exploit systems, and capture flags in a controlled environment. Categories include web application security, cryptography, reverse engineering, and forensics.

Lockpicking Village

Learn the art of physical security bypass. From basic pin tumbler locks to advanced high-security mechanisms, discover how physical security measures can be defeated and how to better protect against these attacks.

Social Engineering Village

Explore the human element of security. Participate in exercises that demonstrate how social engineering attacks work, learn to recognize manipulation techniques, and develop strategies for creating security-aware cultures.

Hardware Hacking

Dive into the world of embedded systems and IoT security. Learn to analyze hardware, identify vulnerabilities in physical devices, and understand how everyday gadgets can become security risks.

Network Forensics

Investigate cyber incidents through network traffic analysis. Learn to identify attack patterns, trace malicious activity, and gather evidence from network communications using industry-standard tools.

AI/ML Security

Explore the cutting-edge challenges of artificial intelligence and machine learning security. Learn about adversarial attacks, model poisoning, and emerging threats in the AI landscape.

Registration & Schedule Information

Challenge registration opens daily at 9:00 AM at each village. Some challenges require pre-registration due to limited capacity. Check the official schedule board for real-time updates and room assignments.

Pro Tip: Arrive early for popular challenges and bring your own laptop with essential tools pre-installed!
