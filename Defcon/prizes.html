Prizes & Benefits

Prizes and Recognition

DEFCON offers a variety of prizes for challenge winners, including exclusive badges, tech gadgets, and sponsored gear. Winning a DEFCON competition is a prestigious achievement that can open doors in the cybersecurity industry.

Exclusive DEFCON Badges

Limited edition electronic badges that are highly coveted collector's items and proof of your DEFCON achievement.

Tech Gadgets

Latest cybersecurity tools, hardware hacking devices, and cutting-edge technology from our sponsors.

Cash Prizes

Substantial monetary rewards for top performers in major competitions and challenge categories.

Sponsored Gear

Professional-grade software licenses, training courses, and equipment from industry-leading companies.

Networking Opportunities

Beyond prizes, DEFCON is renowned for its networking potential. Attendees can meet industry leaders, government officials, and ethical hackers from around the world. The site will provide tips on making connections, attending after-hours events, and joining special interest groups.

Connect with 30,000+ Professionals

DEFCON brings together the largest gathering of cybersecurity professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts in the world. Make connections that will advance your career and expand your knowledge.

Professional Growth

Participating in DEFCON can boost your career by exposing you to the latest trends, tools, and techniques in cybersecurity. The site will feature testimonials from past attendees who have leveraged their DEFCON experience to land jobs, internships, and speaking opportunities.
