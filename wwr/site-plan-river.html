Page 2: Our Rivers

Page Purpose & Content

Page Purpose

The Rivers page will showcase the different rivers available for rafting adventures, highlighting the unique characteristics, difficulty levels, and scenic features of each waterway. This page helps customers choose the perfect river experience based on their skill level and adventure preferences.

Target Audience

Families and adventure seekers looking to understand their options for river rafting experiences, from beginner-friendly floats to challenging whitewater rapids.

Page Content Structure

Required Elements:

  • Consistent header with logo and navigation
  • Page title/hero section
  • At least 3 different rivers with descriptions
  • Difficulty ratings for each river
  • Scenic highlights and key features
  • High-quality river images
  • Consistent footer with contact information

Rivers to Feature

Colorado River

Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate
Length: 5-15 miles
Features: Glenwood Canyon, family-friendly sections, stunning red rock formations

Roaring Fork River

Difficulty: Intermediate to Advanced
Length: 8-12 miles
Features: Technical rapids, pristine wilderness, challenging Class III-IV rapids

Arkansas River

Difficulty: All Levels
Length: 6-18 miles
Features: Diverse sections from gentle floats to extreme whitewater, Royal Gorge

Green River

Difficulty: Beginner
Length: 10-20 miles
Features: Scenic canyon views, wildlife viewing, perfect for first-time rafters

Design Principles Applied

Consistency

The Rivers page maintains the same header, navigation, footer, color scheme, and typography as the home page to ensure brand consistency and user familiarity.

Hierarchy

Clear visual hierarchy with the hero image at top, followed by river sections organized in a grid layout. Each river section uses consistent formatting with difficulty ratings prominently displayed.

Proximity & Alignment

Related information is grouped together within each river section. The grid layout creates clean alignment and makes it easy to compare different river options.

Contrast

Difficulty ratings use star systems and color coding to make skill levels immediately apparent. Images provide visual contrast to text content.

Updated Navigation

Home Rivers Contact Us

The Rivers page serves as the bridge between initial interest (Home page) and taking action (Contact Us page), providing the detailed information customers need to choose their perfect rafting adventure.
